Factory
(COC CSC Fall 2016)
Buttons:

A top-down shooter with a military theme. Grind your way through hoards of zombies and droves of drones as you fight your way to the large boss drone at the end of the level to recapture the factory!

Made in the Unity Engine written in C#, this was the first finished project that I worked on in Computer Science Club. I worked on the death state of the game.
