A 2D Action Platformer where you must fight your way through enemies to obtain the keys to beat the level.

Made in the Unreal Engine written in C++ as a weekend long personal jam game in 4 days. I solo developed the game using a mixture of C++ and Blueprints to do all of the game's programming. I did all of the programming, some of the game's art and SFX as well as level design.
