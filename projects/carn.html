Carnival Cruise Lines is a popular cruise company that offers a wide variety of cruises to destinations around the world. What makes Carnival unique is its emphasis on fun and entertainment, with activities and amenities like water parks, casinos, live shows, and more. Carnival is also known for its affordable prices and family-friendly atmosphere, making it a popular choice for vacationers of all ages. Additionally, Carnival offers a number of themed cruises, such as music festivals and food and wine cruises, to appeal to a diverse range of interests.

The app was made in Unity Engine written in C#. I joined as a developer late in the project primarily working on R&D projects for digital signage and on resolving bugs in the mobile app.
