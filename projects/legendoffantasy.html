Legend of Fantasy
(COC CSC Spring 2019)
Buttons:

A top-down Zelda-like game with a fantasy theme. Fight your way through troves of villainous monsters such as orcs and mushrooms to make your way to the end to defeat the scourge of the Fantaswhere Forest, Orcutoryx.

Made in the Unity Engine written in C#. As my last year as President of the Computer Science Club, I wanted to go out with a bang! I operated as director and lead programmer for this project. I worked on many aspects of the overall game. I programmed the core movement, health systems, animation rigging, boss mob behavior, and many other aspects of the game. You can see my name commented on pretty much every script.
