An Arena FPS game inspired by classic games in the genre such as Halo, Doom, and Portal. A tutorial mode where you make your way through the training facility while learning the ropes of the game. An arena mode where you fight your way through troves of enemies to try to beat your high score.

Made in the Unreal Engine written in C++, I solo developed the game using a mixture of C++ and Blueprints to do all of the game's programming. I did all of the programming, all of the game and level design, all of the UI Art, all of the tutorial's dialogue, some of the SFX, some of the texture art, and edited some free 3D models.
